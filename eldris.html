About ELDRIS

ELDRIS is a price comparison website still in late stages of development.

It works by getting the prices of the user query from the most reputable shopping sites such as Jumia, Konga, Amazon, Ebay and Jiji

It gives the site with the lowest price while also providing the product links for user inspection and scrutiny

It would soon be out in production!!!
